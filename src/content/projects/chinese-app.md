This project explores creating a mobile application designed specifically for heritage learners of Chinese language.

## Background

Heritage learners have unique needs compared to traditional language learners. They often have conversational skills but lack formal reading and writing abilities.

## Approach

The app focuses on bridging the gap between spoken fluency and written literacy, with features designed around real-world usage scenarios.

## Key Features

- Character recognition and practice
- Contextual vocabulary building
- Speaking and listening exercises
- Progress tracking

## Outcome

The prototype demonstrated strong engagement from test users and validated key assumptions about heritage learner needs.
